Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026
🔍
What Information We Collect

Deep Clean Pro is a macOS application that runs entirely on your local device. During normal use, the app only accesses your local file system to perform scanning and cleaning operations. All results remain solely on your own device.

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Your file names, paths, or contents
  • Records or history of cleaning operations
  • Device hardware information or system version
  • Usage behavior or operation logs
⚙️
How We Use Information

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to use, analyze, or share. The app runs independently on your device. All scanning and cleaning logic is completed locally, with no need to communicate with any server.

🛡️
Local Operation and Data Security

All features of Deep Clean Pro run locally, including:

  • Scanning cache, logs, and other junk files
  • Reading file sizes and paths for display purposes
  • Executing deletion operations
  • Storing and reading whitelist rules
Your data belongs only to you. We have no ability and no means to access any files or operation records on your device.
🔗
Third-Party Services

Deep Clean Pro is a purely local application. It does not connect to the internet and does not use any third-party services.

👤
Your Rights

Because we do not hold any of your personal data, you have no need to submit requests for data deletion or export. You have full control over all content on your own device.
