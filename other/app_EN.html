Image Upload to Cloud

Your Smart Assistant for Google Sheets

Introduction

"Image Upload to Cloud" is a powerful tool designed for Google Sheets to help you easily manage image data. Simply provide a spreadsheet containing image links, and the tool will automatically save the images to your Google Drive. It can also optionally perform Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and multilingual translation, writing extracted text directly into your spreadsheet.

Key Features

  • Automatic Image Saving: Batch process image links in Google Sheets and automatically save them to Google Drive for convenient management and long-term storage.
  • Smart OCR Recognition: Automatically recognize text within images and write the content into specified cells.
  • Automatic Translation: Translate OCR-recognized text into Chinese to meet multilingual processing needs.
  • Easy to Use: Simply provide your spreadsheet link and column name to start quickly.
  • Data Security: All operations take place within your Google account. We do not store or access any of your data.

How to Get Started?

  1. Complete OAuth authorization for this Application in the Google Cloud Console.
  2. Install and run this tool within your Google Sheets.
  3. Enter your spreadsheet link and relevant column name, then click "Start."
