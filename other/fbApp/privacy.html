Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

FoodCourt AI operates the intelligent food hall management ecosystem. This document outlines our approach regarding the collection, deployment, and protection of customer and tenant information handled through our platforms

Information Collection

We process data necessary to facilitate transaction completion and system optimization, including

  • Transaction details encompassing items selected, vendor identification, and total expenditure
  • Contact points such as mobile numbers utilized for sending digital order ready alerts
  • Analytical telemetry regarding hardware operational efficiency and UI interaction steps

Data Utilization

The processing of accumulated operational variables serves specific clear functions

  • Routing precise technical instructions to localized kitchen display networks
  • Generating aggregated sales reporting indicators reserved exclusively for landlord visibility
  • Mitigating financial risks through automated security analysis checkpoints

Data Security and Storage

Information fragments are stored across cloud networks featuring standard encryption protocols. Access parameters remain restricted based on operational roles to secure processing integrity. We do not engage in consumer data brokerage activities
